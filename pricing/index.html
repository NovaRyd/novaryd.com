Transparent, upfront fares

NovaRyd shows you the full price before you confirm. No surprises.

How our fares are calculated

  • Base fare: ₹40 covers up to 1.5 km.
  • After that: ₹10.28 per km beyond 1.5 km.
  • GST: 5% added to the ride total at checkout.

Fares may vary based on distance, time, traffic conditions, and demand. You’ll always see the final price before you pay.

Transparency

  • You will always see a live fare breakdown (base fare, distance charges, taxes) before confirming.
  • If any surge or additional fees apply, they’ll be itemized on the checkout screen before payment.

Taxes & fees

  • GST (5%) is applied to the ride total.
  • Any promotional discounts will be shown as a separate line item at checkout.
