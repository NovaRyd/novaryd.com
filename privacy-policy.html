Privacy Policy

At NovaRyd, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, website, and transportation services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account and use our services
  • Make a booking through our mobile application
  • Contact our customer service team
  • Sign up for our newsletter or marketing communications
  • Participate in promotions, surveys, or research

This information may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Billing and payment information
  • Location data (with your permission)
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Communication records between you and NovaRyd

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our mobile application or website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (type, model, operating system)
  • IP address and browser type
  • Usage data and interaction with our services
  • Location data (with your permission)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and fulfill your bookings
  • Communicate with you about your account, bookings, and services
  • Send you marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Personalize your experience and provide tailored content
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or illegal activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, and customer service.

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business.

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing, updating, or deleting your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at pavan@novaryd.com.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
